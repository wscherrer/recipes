white wine cream sauce from salmon oscar recipe, doubled, minus lemon

2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp garlic, chopped
1/2 c. dry white wine
1 c. chicken broth
1 c. heavy cream
__
- Saute garlic in butter until softened. Add wine, bring to a simmer and reduce by half
Add chicken broth and heavy cream, simmer for 8 - 10 minutes or until sauce begin to 
thicken.
